What a Coin!


Changing Bitcoin’s proof-of-work algorithm from SHA256 to Equihash, Bitcoin Platinum will provide an opportunity for countless people around the world to participate in the mining process with widely-available consumer hardware that is manufactured and distributed by reputable mainstream corporations. By increasing the maximum size of the non-witness block from 1MB to 2MB, Bitcoin Platinum will follow the founder’s roadmap of global adoption with on-chain scaling, tend to be the world digital payment network with the low fee pressure.
